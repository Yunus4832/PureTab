# PureTab Privacy Policy

PureTab does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data.

PureTab stores settings locally in your browser, including selected search engine, theme, background image, logo settings, and local search history. These values remain on your device.

If you enable bookmark suggestions, PureTab reads your browser bookmarks locally to show matching suggestions in the new tab search box. Bookmark data is not uploaded or sent to any server.

Searches are sent directly to the search engine you select when you submit a query. PureTab does not proxy, log, or inspect those requests.
